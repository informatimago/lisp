Country and Currency Codes

Country and currency code lists are ISO standards.
Country Codes are defined in ISO 3166-1 and can be purchased from ISO. The ISO 3166/MA Secretariat publishes on their Website the latest, updated ISO country code list in English and French.
The country table published below is complete and up-to-date as of 23rd-12-2003 (according amendment 121). The list can also be downloaded as ACCESS database table from our download page.
